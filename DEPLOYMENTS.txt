Important Note for Protocol Launch (Treasury USDC)
Total USDC Raised in Quick Stats in Hive Control Dashboard = USDC balance of the connected wallet (the treasury address you set when launching the pool).

This is expected and correct behavior.
When users buy tokens, USDC is transferred directly to the treasury address (your wallet or any address you specify).
The pool contract itself ends up with 0 USDC after each buy.
This design is intentional for security and simplicity: funds go straight to the treasury without sitting in the pool.

Make sure to document this clearly in the launch checklist:

When launching a pool, the treasury address should be a secure multisig or dedicated treasury wallet on mainnet.
Quick Stats "Total USDC Raised" reflects the treasury's current USDC balance.
